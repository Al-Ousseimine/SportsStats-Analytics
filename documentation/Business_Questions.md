# Business Questions

## Project Objective

SportsStats aims to analyze over 120 years of Olympic history to uncover meaningful trends that can support journalists and sports analysts.

---

## Business Questions

### 1. Which countries have historically dominated the Olympic Games?

Objective:
Identify the countries with the highest medal counts and understand long-term performance.

---

### 2. Which countries are the most efficient?

Objective:
Compare medals won relative to athlete participation.

---

### 3. How has Olympic participation evolved?

Objective:
Measure the evolution of athlete participation over time.

---

### 4. How are Olympic medals distributed?

Objective:
Analyze the distribution of Gold, Silver and Bronze medals.

---

### 5. Which insights can support business stakeholders?

Objective:
Provide actionable insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.
